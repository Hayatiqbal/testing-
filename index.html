Shopify Fashion Brand

Klaviyo Email Automation Overhaul

Complete Klaviyo setup and automation strategy for a growing DTC fashion brand struggling with low email revenue.

Challenge

The brand had no email automation in place. Email contributed less than 5% of total revenue, with high cart abandonment and zero post-purchase engagement.

Strategy

Implemented a full Klaviyo setup with advanced segmentation, welcome series, abandoned cart flow, browse abandonment, and post-purchase sequences.

Execution

Designed 25+ custom email templates, built 8 automated flows, and set up weekly campaign calendar with A/B testing across subject lines and send times.

Results

Within 90 days, email revenue increased from 5% to 32% of total store revenue, with abandoned cart recovery rate improving by 340%.

DTC Skincare Brand

Omnisend Migration & Growth Strategy

Platform migration from Mailchimp to Omnisend with a complete retention marketing strategy for a premium skincare brand.

Challenge

The brand was losing subscribers due to poor segmentation on Mailchimp, resulting in high unsubscribe rates and declining engagement metrics across all campaigns.

Strategy

Migrated to Omnisend with clean data, implemented RFM-based segmentation, designed personalized product recommendation flows, and launched a VIP loyalty program via email.

Execution

Created custom segments for skincare routines, built personalized product recommendation engine, and designed 6 automated flows with dynamic content blocks.

Results

Customer repeat purchase rate doubled within 60 days. Unsubscribe rates dropped by 65%, and email-attributed revenue grew by 280% quarter over quarter.
